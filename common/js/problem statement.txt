This research focuses on developing an AI-powered quiz website that enhances learning and assessment. The platform generates dynamic questions using AI, ensuring a diverse and adaptive learning experience across multiple subjects. Instant feedback helps users identify mistakes and improve in real time, while automated certificate generation provides credibility to assessments. Restricted access during quizzes ensures fairness and prevents malpractice. By studying these features, this research aims to improve digital learning effectiveness, engagement, and security in online assessments.
